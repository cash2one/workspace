Strategic Sourcing Alters Supply Chain Landscape

3/28/2017 // Victoria Kickham in: Industry

Supply chain risk is taking on a larger role in electronics purchasing as geopolitical, environmental and other factors weigh heavy on buyers minds when sourcing new products. The trend continues in 2017, cementing a gradual change in sourcing from low-cost regionsespecially China. As organizations seek to diversify their supply chains and reduce risks by sourcing closer to where their products are made, sourcing from China is less about cost and more about strategy. Read More
